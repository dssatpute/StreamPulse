# StreamPulse

StreamPulse is a product analytics platform for a streaming media business.

At a high level, it captures user behavior in near real time and turns it into business-ready insights such as:

- What content is trending now
- How users engage with content over sessions
- Search and click behavior patterns
- Early churn risk signals

## What the product is about

The goal of StreamPulse is to help business teams make faster and better decisions by providing a clear view of customer behavior.

It connects raw activity data (clicks, playback actions, and profile updates) to reporting outputs that product, growth, and retention teams can use directly.

## What it utilizes at a high level

- Databricks for data processing and pipeline orchestration
- Azure Event Hubs for live event streaming
- Azure Data Lake Storage for dimension and historical data storage
- Python-based simulators to generate realistic platform activity
- Layered data model (Bronze, Silver, Gold) for reliable analytics

## Business value

With StreamPulse, teams can:

- Monitor platform engagement continuously
- Identify high-interest content quickly
- Track conversion behavior from search to interaction
- Spot churn risk earlier and take retention actions
